### Holder Behavior Categories

| **Type**     | **Hold Time**     | **What It Means**                                                                 | **Exit Prediction**                                          |
|--------------|-------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| **SNIPER**   | < 1 minute        | Bot/MEV/sandwich attack behavior. Instant entry and exit, usually automated.      | Will exit current token within minutes of profit             |
| **SCALPER**  | 1-5 minutes       | Ultra-fast manual/semi-automated scalping. Snipes launches and dumps immediately. | Will exit within 1-5 minutes or on the first green candle    |
| **MOMENTUM** | 5-30 minutes      | Short-term momentum trader. Rides initial pumps and exits before consolidation.   | Will exit within 5-30 minutes, likely on the first fade      |
| **INTRADAY** | 30 min - 4 hours  | Short-term trader riding multi-hour moves within the same session.                | Will exit within 1-4 hours and avoid holding overnight       |
| **DAY_TRADER** | 4-24 hours     | Full-day trader who enters and exits positions within a 24-hour window.           | Will exit within 24 hours, high risk of overnight dump       |
| **SWING**    | 1-7 days          | Multi-day swing trader. Holds through daily volatility seeking bigger moves.      | Will exit within a week, moderate holder risk                |
| **POSITION** | 7-30 days         | Position trader with conviction lasting weeks but not months.                     | Will exit within 30 days, lower but notable risk             |
| **HOLDER**   | 30+ days          | Long-term conviction holder who rides through major volatility.                   | Likely to hold indefinitely, lowest exit risk                |
